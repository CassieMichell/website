Metal Head Welding and Fabrication was started in 2019 by Adam Bader.
Adam is a Red Seal Welder with a decade of experience as a profession tradesman. He has worked
in shops, remote worksites across Canada and shutdowns for Sawmills, Mines and Pulpmills.
Adam is a continuous learner and continuously challenging himself by taking advanced courses to
increase his knowledgebase.

Adam is from Kamloops and spends his freetime enjoying the outdoors camping and chasing after his one year old son.
